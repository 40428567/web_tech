Jack waited for the giant to fall asleep and quietly, trying not to wake the giant, started to get out of hiding. Sneaking along the wall, Jack accidentally touched the harp, and it loudly crashed to the floor.

Jack was overwhelmed by the great fear that the giant would now wake up, and he desperately ran away. Reaching the cloud, he quickly jumped on the stalk and started to climb down.

Here, under the cloud, it was so calm that he could not believe in what trouble he was. On the way down, Jack wondered how many beans were on the beanstalk…
