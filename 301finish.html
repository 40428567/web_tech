Jack returned to home without selling the cow, which upset his mother. But Jack convinced her to try making cheese according to a new recipe.

In the 6 months, Jack worked hard hoping for cheese ripening. And what a great surprise it was when he sold the first batch of cheese in a blink of an eye. After a while, Jack managed to buy more cows and make more money on cheese.

And so Jack, his mother lived happy ever after.
